What about the "Challenge" part of this?

The challenge is for you to design your own Maker Coin!

The rules are:

  1. Do not spend any money on a 3D printer (I will print your project)
  2. Do not spend any money on software (free versions are sufficient)
  3. Ask an adult for help/permission installing software
  4. In total this is a challenging project. There is no rush. Try your best not to get frustrated, stick with it

Fusion 360 is very powerful, and it's popular both with hobbyists and professionals. Brian used it for the pill bottle. Angus will walk you thru how to design your Maker Coin in Fusion 360. If you cannot install software on your computer, or if you already know and like TinkerCad, this is a perfectly fine alternative

Angus uses InkScape in his tutorial to add a logo, please use whatever art software you are comfortable with
